Linguistics

I'm a huge fan of linguistics, many thanks to an academic summer camp (shoutout to CTY!) a few years back that got me absolutely hooked. I have gone on to take many classes at Columbia with respect to this subject, as well as creating my own ConLang (constructed language) and writing a couple of papers as part of assignments.


My projects/papers/the like:

  • Thabulou - a ConLang by a person whose entire concept of language was just English

  • PhonPhon - Russian [I] vs. [i] distinction in nonnative English speakers

  • Balti - multiple papers done as part of a Language Documentation: Field Methods course, taught by the wonderful Dr. Meredith Landman
